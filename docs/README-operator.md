# Operator Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the SnapNShop Operations team! Your job is to ensure every customer gets their order on time.

### System Access
* **Admin Dashboard:** `https://yourdomain.com/admin`
* **Login:** [Ask Manager for credentials]

### Your Daily Checklist
1.  **09:00 AM:** Log in and filter for **"Pending"** orders.
2.  **09:15 AM:** Export the CSV and email it to our IndiaMART supplier.
3.  **09:30 AM:** Mark those orders as **"Placed"** in the dashboard.
4.  **02:00 PM:** Check email for Tracking Numbers (AWBs) from the supplier.
5.  **02:15 PM:** Find the "Placed" orders and click **"Add Tracking"**. Enter the AWB.
6.  **05:00 PM:** Check for any "Stalled" orders (Pending > 24 hours) and escalate.

### Tools You Need
* **WhatsApp Web:** To chat with suppliers.
* **Gmail/Outlook:** To send POs.
* **Excel/Sheets:** To view the exported CSVs.
